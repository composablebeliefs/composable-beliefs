# Household cashflow

Synthesized view of monthly money movement. Maintained in place as new
[source](2026-q2-budget-source.md) captures land — this is Layer-2 synthesis, not a
capture.

## Monthly shape
- **In:** ~$9,200 net.
- **Fixed out:** ~$3,570 (housing $2,650, utilities+insurance $740, subscriptions $180).
- **Variable out:** ~$2,200 (groceries+household $1,300, discretionary $900).
- **Automatic savings:** $1,500.
- **Net free after savings:** ~$1,930/mo.

## Runway
Liquid reserve ~$34,000 against ~$5,770 of non-savings monthly outflow → **~5.9 months**
of bare-essentials runway if income stopped (excludes the discretionary $900, which is
cuttable).
